# ReadEz Feature Specifications

This document outlines all planned features for ReadEz, organized by category.

---

## Core Reading

### PDF Support
Ability to open, read, and navigate PDF files with high-quality rendering. Supports standard PDF features including table of contents navigation and page thumbnails.

### EPUB Support
Support for reading EPUB format ebooks with reflowable text that adapts to screen size. Handles both EPUB2 and EPUB3 specifications including embedded fonts and images.

### Cloud Sync
Synchronization of books, reading progress, and user preferences across multiple devices using cloud storage. Basic sync provides essential syncing capabilities while priority sync offers faster and more reliable synchronization.

### Storage Limit
Maximum amount of cloud storage space allocated for storing user's ebook files and related data. Determines how many books and associated files can be stored in the cloud.

### Books Limit
Maximum number of individual books that can be stored in a user's library. This limit applies to the total count of books regardless of file size.

### Offline Reading
Ability to read books without an internet connection by caching book content locally on the device. Users can access previously opened books even when offline.

### Multi-device Sync
Capability to access the same library and reading progress across multiple devices simultaneously. Ensures seamless transitions between devices like phone, tablet, and computer.

---

## Enhanced Features

### Annotations & Highlights
Tools for marking up text with highlights in different colors and adding personal notes or comments to specific passages. All annotations are saved and synced across devices.

### Bookmarks
Feature to mark specific pages or locations in a book for quick reference later. Users can add multiple bookmarks per book and jump directly to bookmarked locations.

### Reading Progress Tracking
System that tracks how much of each book has been read, including page numbers and percentage completion. Advanced tracking includes reading speed, time spent per session, and estimated time to finish.

### Collections/Libraries
Organizational system for grouping books into custom categories or collections based on genre, subject, or personal preferences. Helps manage large libraries with multiple organizational schemes.

### Custom Themes
Visual appearance options for the reading interface including background colors, text colors, and overall aesthetic styling. Advanced options allow users to define custom CSS for complete visual control.

### Font Options
Selection of typefaces and typography settings for reading text. Basic options include common fonts and sizes, extended offers more font families, and full control provides complete typography customization including line height and letter spacing.

### Sharing & Collaboration
Features that enable users to share annotations, notes, or entire books with others. Full collaboration allows multiple users to work together on annotations and discussions within books.

---

## AI-Powered Features

### AI Book Summaries
Automated generation of concise summaries of entire books using artificial intelligence. Provides quick overviews of book content without reading the full text.

### AI Chapter Summaries
AI-generated summaries for individual chapters within a book. Helps users recall chapter content or preview what's ahead.

### AI Q&A
Interactive feature allowing users to ask questions about book content and receive AI-generated answers based on the text. Useful for studying, research, or clarifying complex concepts.

### Smart Search
Semantic search capability that understands the meaning and context of search queries, not just keyword matching. Finds relevant passages even when exact words don't match the query.

### Translation
Language translation features for books or selected text passages. Basic translation covers common languages while advanced supports more languages and better accuracy.

---

## Marketplace

### Browse Marketplace
Interface for exploring a curated collection of ebooks available through the platform. View-only access allows browsing titles and descriptions without downloading.

### Download Free Books
Ability to download and add free books from the marketplace directly to the user's library. Includes public domain works and books offered freely by authors.

### Purchase Premium Books
Integration with payment systems to buy premium ebooks through the marketplace. Purchased books are automatically added to the user's library.

### Author Connect
Feature enabling direct communication or connection between readers and book authors. Includes author profiles, updates, and potential for direct messaging.

### Early Access Books
Access to books before their official public release date. Allows readers to preview upcoming titles and provide feedback to authors.

---

## Analytics & Insights

### Basic Reading Stats
Simple metrics showing total books read, pages completed, and overall reading activity. Provides fundamental insights into reading habits.

### Reading Time Tracking
Detailed tracking of time spent reading each book and overall reading time. Records active reading sessions and calculates daily, weekly, and monthly totals.

### Reading Goals
System for setting personal reading targets such as books per month or pages per day. Tracks progress toward goals and provides motivational feedback.

### Advanced Analytics Dashboard
Comprehensive visualization of reading data including charts, graphs, and detailed breakdowns of reading patterns. Offers deep insights into reading habits over time.

### Reading Patterns & Insights
Analysis of when, where, and how users read, identifying trends and patterns. Provides insights like most productive reading times or average session lengths.

### Export Data
Functionality to download all personal reading data in standard formats for backup or analysis in external tools. Ensures users maintain ownership of their data.

### Productivity Reports
Periodic summaries of reading activity with metrics on efficiency, consistency, and achievement of reading goals. Helps users understand and optimize their reading habits.

---

## Support & Other

### Ads
Advertising policy for the application. ReadEz maintains an ad-free experience across all tiers.

### Support
Level of customer support provided to users. Ranges from community-based help forums to direct email support with guaranteed response times and priority handling for urgent issues.
